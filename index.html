CANNY

For the Canny I chose to use the thresholds 100,450 because I believed that it gave me a good indicator of strong lines. I checked with different types and when I used thresholds like 800,1000 there were very little lines. When I used 40,100, I was getting a lot of lines. So when it had the smaller thresholds it was noticing everything. So I chose to have 100,450 because I only wanted to show the really strong edges. I did my testing with an image of the plants and window; my threshold was about to see the edge of the window, the plant against the sky and the door on the right.
